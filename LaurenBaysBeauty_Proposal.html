Overview

Your brand deserves a home as beautiful as your results.

"You've built something exceptional on Instagram. Now let's build the website that turns those followers into appointments."

Lauren Bays Beauty offers some of the most advanced aesthetic treatments available — from BBL laser and HALO resurfacing to custom microneedling and RF skin tightening. The website needs to earn that same level of trust and sophistication at first glance.


This proposal outlines a fully custom website: designed in your brand's earthy, warm palette, written with copy that educates and converts, and structured to make booking effortless from any device.

Scope of Work

Everything included,
nothing left out.

01

Design System

Custom color palette, typography pairing (Cormorant Garamond + Jost), button styles, spacing system, and photo direction — all documented for long-term brand consistency.

02

16 Pages Built

Home, About (with provider listings), 13 service-specific pages, and a Contact page — each with custom layout, copy, and imagery direction tailored to the service.

Benefit-led copy written for every page — hero headlines, service descriptions, what-to-expect sections, and CTAs — optimized for both the reader and search engines.

04

Mangomint Integration

Your Mangomint booking link woven throughout the site — nav button, hero CTAs, service pages, and contact page — so the path to booking is never more than one click away.

Page titles, meta descriptions, URL slugs, image alt text, and Google Search Console setup — so your site starts building organic visibility from day one.

06

Mobile-First Build

Every page reviewed and optimized for mobile. With the majority of aesthetic clientele discovering services on their phones, a flawless mobile experience is non-negotiable.
