The Collaborations
Bulova × Shelby
Racing Chronograph · 150th Anniversary
Celebrating Bulova's auto racing heritage with a limited-edition tribute to the iconic 1967 Shelby GT500 — 7,500 pieces worldwide, powered by the Bulova Precisionist movement.
  • 43mm bullhead chronograph in Shelby's signature blue & white colorway
  • Sapphire crystal, Carroll Shelby Cobra icon at 3 o'clock, numbered case back
  • Precisionist movement: 262kHz accuracy to 1/1000th of a second
  • 10–15 units secured exclusively for Dallas Sold Out Nationally
  • Aaron Shelby in-person appearance — live engagement moment
  • Texas Team Shelby vehicles on-site — immersive brand environment
  • Includes 1:24 scale die-cast GT500 model in special presentation box
Bulova × Stetson
American West Collection · 6666 Airstream
Two pillars of American craftsmanship united by shared family history — Stetson (est. 1865) and Bulova (est. 1875) — and a commitment to philanthropy through the Bulova Stetson Fund.
  • 39mm automatic with exhibition case back, domed sapphire, onion crown with black onyx cabochon
  • Two models: The Bison and The Longhorn — each $750 MSRP
  • Stetson 6666 Airstream serves as the visual centerpiece in the parking lot queue
  • Provided by Vogt RV / Airstream of DFW — merchandised with Stetson timepieces + hat raffle
  • Bulova × Stetson collab watch raffle for attendees
  • $50K donation to Veterans Watchmaker Initiative woven into brand story

Why This Model Scales
The Architecture Is Repeatable
  • The Airstream is mobile — this model replicates at any WindUp city or lifestyle event
  • The Conviction Buyer framework is brand-agnostic: any Bulova partner with a strong lifestyle identity can plug in
  • City-specific artwork (Alex Asfour) builds a scalable visual identity system for future markets
This Is Not a Watch Fair Activation
  • It is a brand environment that happens to be at a watch fair — that distinction determines who walks up
  • Aaron Shelby's presence and Team Shelby cars create earned media that no booth spend can buy
  • Editorial amplification via Worn & Wound converts a one-day event into multi-week brand content
